It is important to understand what information you need to provide in order for our team to finish processing your order for a SSL Certificate.

You must make sure that the following items consistently match in order to have your server certificate order processed:

  1. The Registrant listed in the InterNIC/WHOIS records for the domain name in question
  2. The Organization appearing in your "Proof of Organization" documents or DUNS number
  3. The Organization entered into the CSR (Certificate Signing Request)

If any of the above items do not match or are not submitted, the processing of your certificate request will be delayed further.

If you were instructed to submit additional documentation, please submit your Proof of Organization information and InterNIC record by email to docs@ssl.com or fax 1-832-201-7706. Please include your business and domain name in all communications.

Acceptable documents for Proof of Organization include:

  • DUNS number (Dun and Bradstreet).
  • Articles of Incorporation
  • Business License
  • Doing Business As (DBA) registration
  • Partnership documentation
  • Sole Proprietorship documentation


If you represent a Government Department, Non-Profit Organization, or a University, then you should email us at info@ssl.com

Note: All documents must be provided in English or with an English translation.


All of your information must be received by SSL before your order can be completed. Once you have submitted these documents, they will not be required again unless the organization or common name changes.

In addition, we may contact your state or local government to confirm that your organization is in "Good Standing".
